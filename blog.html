11/10/2024

So I decided to start this blog over. The last time it felt like just a bunch of angsty ramblings. This time I want to put at least a bit more effort into what actually goes here.

This thing is publicly accessible so I don’t want to use this as a private diary anymore. I still don’t think anyone reads this, but some shit should stay private. Even though I want to get it off my chest somehow.

I want to try turning this into a kind of study of my artistic process and all this related to that. My thoughts about projects that I’m working on, ideas, inspirations and all that.

For example, recently I started seeing this tattoo artist by the name of Ladybug and I think I kinda got hooked on getting tattoos. It hasn’t even been two months yet and I’ve already got six tattoos. At first I wasn’t sure if I wanted to get more because I liked getting tattooed or because I found her attractive. And even after asking her out and getting rejected I still want to get more. And to be honest I’m still not sure if it’s just the tattooing itself or the whole shabang. One way or another my mood has been up since I started that. I’m starting to get back into working on stuff. Small stuff so far but better than nothing. Finally got to work on nDies (Mathaels new name) profile pic and banner. Planning a little Halloween stream on the Anarchy channel just to kind of keep it alive a bit. And I’ve really been feeling like getting back into music, maybe even making and album or an EP.
